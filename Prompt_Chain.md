\# Prompt Chain Document — Task 1



\## Topic

“I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened”

Genre: Productivity / Lifestyle



---



\## Prompt 1 — Audience \& Angle Analysis

You are a fast-growing YouTube creator who deeply understands audience retention, modern productivity culture, and AI trends.



Analyze the topic:

“I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened”



Define:

1\. The target audience (age, mindset, interests)

2\. The emotional hook that will make viewers click

3\. What makes this video different from generic “AI productivity” videos

4\. The core message the viewer should walk away with



Keep the analysis concise but insightful.



---



\## Prompt 2 — Title Generation

Using the audience and angle defined above, generate 7 YouTube video titles for the topic:



“I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened”



Constraints:

\- Titles must feel human, not exaggerated or clickbait

\- Avoid generic phrases like “you won’t believe” or “shocking”

\- Optimize for curiosity and relatability

\- Max 60 characters per title



Rank the titles from strongest to weakest based on click potential.



---



\## Prompt 3 — Full Script Generation

Write a full YouTube video script for the topic:

“I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened”



Guidelines:

\- First 7 seconds must strongly hook the viewer

\- Maintain a conversational, reflective, and modern tone

\- Structure the script into:

&nbsp; - Hook

&nbsp; - Setup

&nbsp; - Morning

&nbsp; - Midday

&nbsp; - Evening

&nbsp; - Reflection

&nbsp; - Ending thought

\- Avoid clichés and generic productivity advice

\- Include subtle pattern breaks and emotional shifts

\- Script length: ~2.5–3 minutes



The script should feel like a real human experiment, not an AI demo.



---



\## Prompt 4 — Script Refinement

Review the script you just generated.



Refine it by:

\- Tightening slow sections

\- Improving transitions between time blocks

\- Enhancing emotional depth without adding length

\- Ensuring every section gives the viewer a reason to keep watching



Do not rewrite from scratch.

Improve clarity, pacing, and authenticity.
