\# Task 1 — Prompt Chain



\## Step 1: Topic Analysis

Analyze the topic: "I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened".

Define target audience, emotional hook, uniqueness, and core takeaway.



\## Step 2: Title Generation

Generate multiple YouTube titles optimized for curiosity and relatability without clickbait.



\## Step 3: Script Writing

Write a 2.5–3 minute YouTube video script with a strong hook, structured timeline, and reflective ending.



\## Step 4: Script Refinement

Improve pacing, transitions, and emotional depth without changing length.



\## Step 5: Thumbnail Concept

Design a high-performing thumbnail concept including text, emotion, and visual strategy.



Each step is designed to guide AI outputs systematically rather than relying on single prompts.
